Why Use Us

Professional pet sitting, dog walking and Pet Supplies services.

Durham House of Pets Ltd is the UK’s longest, established National pet sitting and pet care franchise, offering a comprehensive pet care service and Products to thousands of customers every year. Durham House of Pets operates as a franchise, with the first franchise awarded in 2001 and now has over 50 franchisees running their own businesses across England, Scotland and Wales, under the Durham House of Pets model.

For your peace of mind all of our Petpals owners and their carers are DBS checked, uniformed and fully insured. Petpals has been a member of the bfa (British Franchise Association) since 2001. We fully support the bfa’s commitment to professional, ethical and sustainable franchising.
